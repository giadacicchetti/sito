about giada


giada (b. 1997) is an Italian visual artist based in Gent and Roma. She graduated from the BA in Photography at Rome University of Fine Arts (RUFA) in 2019, and she's currently attenting the Master in Photography at Royal Academy of Fine Arts Gent (KASK). She’s co-founder of the photographic collective Ammodo Studio, where she works as photographer and videomaker. In 2020 she worked as assistant for Samuel Gratacap, following the filming of his first film “Un tipo strano” as second assistant director. she’s currently Michele Palazzi assistant.

giada believes in storytelling magical power: her projects always try to deal subtely with the spell of narration and images, looking back to their original forms. she’s fascinated by how our reality is intertwined to the story we make of it. she works mainly with photography but her work often involves different mediums (film, text, installation)
Her practice involves a strong interest in curating photography and bookmaking, she curated three exhibitions for RUFA Open Days and the collective exhibition "And All is Always Now" from the students of the 3rd BA from KASK.
