beste giada

2020


Beste giada is a 756cm long Leporello.
The work began as an exploration of the spaces of a new city, where I moved in a time of pandemic.
Photography was a tool and a game to create contact with an empty city where life was on a pause for everyone.
Beste giada is the diary of a journey and a quarantine, of the visual and verbal notes, walking in the streets, looking around, and constructing a new gaze. Rediscovering the banality of everyday objects by fitting them in different forms and with new names was the game with which photography was the bridge between my reality and that new space to be discovered only without touching.
In contrast with the solitude of the pandemic, the playfulness of the leporello with its drawings and notes has been a way to re-discover the semplicity of the photographic apparatus, and the hirony it hides.
