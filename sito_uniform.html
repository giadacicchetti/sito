uniform

2017

adj.
1.remaining the same in all cases and at all times; unchanging in form or character.
2. denoting a garment forming part of a person’s uniform.

noun
3. the distinctive clothing worn by members of the same organization or body or by
children attending certain schools.
With this project I tried to look at how we build our identity during adolescence and how we are used to see each other. In this scenery I used the uniform as a metaphore, questioning about what really resides in differences and why and how we approach difference in specific ways. I worked with two Irish twins, conversating with them and trying to find out their way of seeing themselves as individuals, playing with their ralation as twins, but with very different aspects, and using their school uniform as metaphore to talk about the relation between appearence and identity.
